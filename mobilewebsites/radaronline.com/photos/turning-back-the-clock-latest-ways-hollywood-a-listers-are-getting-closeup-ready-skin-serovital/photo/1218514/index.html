Celebrities will go to great lengths to look and feel young when rocking the red carpet, which is why many of them opt for eccentric facials or procedures to look ready for the camera all the time! From vampire facials to high-tech lasers, find out how your favorite A-listers are turning back the clock!
Kim Kardashian received a creepy vampire facial on an episode of Kim and Kourtney Take Miami. Doctors injected needles filled with her blood into her face by using a tool called Dermapen. Although the reality star was left in tears from the pain, she couldn’t get enough of the results!
The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star also swears by a procedure that’s a lot less bloody: Fraxel! The high-tech laser reduces wrinkles by removing damaged skin cells. “I’m like, girls, you should go get a Fraxel laser on your face,” she told Oprah Winfrey. “I’m really open about telling all my friends and my sisters what the best treatments are.”
Some celebrities want their skin to be frozen in time – literally! Jennifer Aniston is a fan of face-freezing treatments to stop wrinkles from forming. A device full of liquid nitrogen is placed next to nerves in an attempt to freeze muscles and stop lines from appearing.
Angelina Jolie defies aging by skipping out on the beauty treatments! Instead she opts for caviar skin cream, which contains the eggs of Baerii sturgeon to produce moisturized and firm skin.
Bar Refaeli is covered in gold – and we’re not talking about jewelry! The model received a Caviar and Carat Facial that contains caviar, sea buckthorn, honey and, of course, gold.
Victoria Beckham is aging in reverse – and it’s all thanks to bird poop! The singer-turned-fashion designer is a regular of Japanese Geisha Facials, which use nightingale poo to cleanse skin.
Getty Images
If you thought bird poop was odd, then you’ll be shocked to know that Jennifer Lopez uses anti-aging placenta masks!
Beauty is pain! Gwyneth Paltrow loves the laser treatment Thermage. “It’s quite painful,” she told Harper’s Bazaar. “It feels like someone’s smacking your face with a rubber band that has an electric shock in it. But I would do it again, because I feel like it took five years off my face.”
Naomi Campbell doesn’t look a day over 40 (she’s 44!) – and it’s all thanks to microdermabrasion! The procedure exfoliates skin with crystals.
Kate Hudson is also a fan of crystals on her skin. The actress uses Goldfaden MD Doctor’s Scrub, which is an exfoliator made from ruby crystals, organic red tea extracts and seaweed.
Corbis
Do you want to look younger than you are like these ageless celebrities? Then turn back the clock with the anti-aging product SeroVital! The body produces hGH, which is a hormone that increases the production of collagen to help your skin maintain its structure. But hGH and collagen slow down once people age, causing loose skin to form. Thats where SeroVital comes in! The product contains amino acids that naturally boost hGH when the pill is taken on an empty stomach. Serovital can increase hGH levels by up to 682 percent!

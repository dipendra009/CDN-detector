Staples has what you need to make business, school work and organization easier. More than 2,000 locations worldwide provide shoppers with everything from supplies and office furniture to copy and printing services.

Staples Discount Tips

With a plethora of printable and online coupons, Stapes gives you plenty of ways to save, often to the tune of 25 to 50 percent off certain products, or even whole departments. Plus, you can use multiple coupon codes on a single order for additional savings, and they will price match if you find a product cheaper somewhere else. Staples.com has a Daily Deals page, as well as a coupon page with various ways to save, but just check here frequently to get all the latest specials in once place.

Save even more at Staples by signing up for emails to get $10 off a $50 purchase. Alternatively, sign up for text messages and earn $5 off your next $25 purchase. If you run a small business or you’re stocking up for back to school, Staples has you covered with their Rewards program, which offers 5 percent back in rewards on everything you buy, free shipping and $2 back every time you recycle your old printer ink cartridges. Staples Rewards will also earn you bonus rewards and exclusive offers once you spend a certain amount. Plus, if you’re a teacher, you can enroll in their Teacher Rewards Program and get an additional 10 percent back (that’s on top of the regular 5 percent) in rewards. Finally, while Rewards members will always get free shipping, you can also save on shipping costs by spending at least $45 or by placing an order at one of their in-store kiosks.

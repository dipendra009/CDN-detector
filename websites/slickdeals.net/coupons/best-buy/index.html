More than 70 percent of the country lives within 15 minutes of a Best Buy, so its no wonder they are one of the leading providers of tech products and services. Best Buy generates $40 billion a year, all while offering some of the best deals on electronics, appliances and media products.

Best Buy Discount Tips

With separate Deal pages for Weekly Specials, Deals of the Day, Hottest Deals, College Student Deals and holiday deals (you can’t beat their Black Friday Deals,) it’s safe to say that Best Buy is all about getting you a great deal. Bookmark this page and we’ll keep you up to date on the best promotions they have to offer, which often range from 20 to 60 percent off certain departments, as well as dollar amounts off specific products. In fact, it’s not unusual to see $20, $50 or even hundreds of dollars off certain electronics. Plus, if you find a product cheaper somewhere else, (including BestBuy.com, which often has lower prices than in the store) Best Buy will match the price.

You’ll find significant savings on the Best Buy Open Box page as well. If you’re willing to take a display model or returned product, you can often save at least $50 or even up to $500 on things like laptops, TVs and home theaters. Students can also save big at Best Buy with the College Student Deals program, which gives students special discounts and emailed coupon codes. If you're moving you can take advantage of the Movers Coupon for 10% off your next purchase.

If you always got to have the latest gadgets and electronics, you’ll definitely want to join the My Best Buy rewards program. It’s free to join and you’ll earn rewards points for every dollar spent, free shipping on orders over $35, plus access to exclusive sales. For even more savings, sign up for one of Best Buy’s credit cards, which will get you sign-on rewards bonuses, as well as rewards points on everything you buy.
